Japanese Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ½ apple
  • 1½ lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • freshly ground black pepper

For The Curry Sauce

  • 1½ Tbsp neutral oil
  • 4 cups chicken stock/broth
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 package Japanese curry roux

For Serving

  • 8 servings cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • Japanese red pickled vegetables

Preparation Time

Prep Time:20mins | Cook Time: 50mins | Total Time: 1h 10mins

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients. Please read my blog post on the “secret ingredient” we add to the curry. For this recipe, I add ginger, garlic, apple, honey, soy sauce, and ketchup.
